Bamboo

Description:

Bamboo is the fastest growing land plant in the world, with some species growing up to 1.5 inches per hour and is mostly native to china.

Details:

Attribute Information
Family Poaceae
Class Liliopsida
Species There are over 1000+ species
Leaf Type Blade
Scientific Name Bambusoideae (subfamily)
Age 3-7 years
Height 40 metres
Flower Small, inconspicuous spikes
Fruit Bamboo fruits are small, dry, and often contained within capsules or spikelets
